Introduction

Learn the basics of cooking meat in Chinese style.

Ingredients

  • Beef or Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Spring Onions
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Chinese Five Spice

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat with soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and Chinese five spice.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the marinated meat and cook until browned.
  4. Add spring onions and stir-fry until cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
