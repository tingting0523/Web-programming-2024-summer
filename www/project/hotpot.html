Hotpot Preparation Steps

1

Step One: Prepare Ingredients

Gather all the necessary ingredients including meats, vegetables, and hotpot sauces. Ensure everything is fresh and ready to cook.

2

Step Two: Set Up the Hotpot

Place the hotpot on a heat source and add the broth. Allow it to heat up until it reaches a boil.

3

Step Three: Cook Ingredients

Start adding the prepared ingredients into the hotpot. Cook them until they are tender and fully cooked.

4

Step Four: Enjoy Your Hotpot

Once everything is cooked, serve the hotpot with dipping sauces and enjoy your meal with family and friends.
